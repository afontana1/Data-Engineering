Do you also have a song stuck in your head but don’t know the lyrics and therefore cannot identify which song is after the peace of your mind? Well, now you can find out, thanks to a neat Google (NASDAQ:GOOGL) (NASDAQ:GOOG) feature.





What Happened: Renowned tech influencer Marques Brownlee shared a hidden Google Assistant feature that allows users to figure out which song has been stuck in their heads. 





The YouTuber has 16.8 million followers on the platform and shared a video titled “A Hidden Google Assistant Feature!” earlier this month. 





See Also: Think You Know Your iPhone Inside-Out? Marques Brownlee Reveals 3 Hidden Features





In the video, Brownlee said users usually use the Shazam app when they hear a song in the background, on the radio, or in a mall. 





He added that people who watch tech videos will know that Google Pixel also has a Now Playing feature, which can show the name of a song playing in the device’s environment in real time. 





Then he asks, “But did you know that you can sing a song yourself and Google can still identify that?”





According to Brownlee, users don’t need to use actual words while singing a song, they can just hum or whistle and Google Assistant will identify the songs that match the same.





This feature also works the same way on iPhones but “looks kinda cooler.”
