• Ford Motor Co (NYSE:F) has unveiled Lincoln’s newest SUV, the all-new 2024 Nautilus, with new features and connected experiences.

  • The Nautilus was designed and engineered for the brand’s younger, global customers, offering them choices in technology, design, and powertrains based on their needs and lifestyles.

  • Clients can enable Android Auto and Apple Inc (NASDAQ:AAPL) Apple CarPlay features through wireless connections.

  • Alexa Built-in makes it easy to control Nautilus features and smart home devices with the same voice commands clients use at home.

  • Nautilus provides Advanced Driver Assistance System (ADAS) capabilities and has Lincoln BlueCruise 1.2, which improves hands-free driving with new features.

  • Also Read: Ford Pumps $1.3B To Transform 70-Year-Old Canada Assembly Plant Into EV-Making Hub

  • Lane Change Assist enables the Nautilus to perform a hands-free lane change when requested by the driver by tapping the turn signal.

  • The vehicle offers two powertrains, a 2.0-liter turbocharged engine as well as a hybrid powertrain.

  • Nautilus will arrive in North America showrooms in early 2024 while ordering for the SUV is already open.

  • Price Action: F shares are trading higher by 0.63% at $12.76 in premarket on the last check Tuesday.
